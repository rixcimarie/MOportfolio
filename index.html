About Me



Senior User Experience Designer with a passion for designing clean and functional user experiences. Currently working at SunTrust banks as a part of the EXD Team to create and design meaningful financial experiences for customers. I have a masters degree in Computer Science (HCI) from Georgia State University & I enjoy turning complex problems into beautiful and intuitive interface designs.



Prior to this, I worked as a principal UX Designer for MNC Unisys Corporation for 2.5 years and created end to end User Experiences for Fortune 500 clients and businesses all over the world.



In my free time, I write articles related to design systems, processes et., Thrillers and FIFA keep me alive. Also, a full-time travel Jedi and a pizza fanatic.
